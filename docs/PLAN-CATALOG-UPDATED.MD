### 🔧 Technical SEO Health

**Full Site Audit**
We scan your entire website looking for technical problems that
are silently hurting your rankings. Most site owners have no idea
these issues exist until they see them listed out.

**Missing Titles & Descriptions Finder**
Find every page on your site that's missing a meta title or
description — Google's first impression of your page.
Fix them all with one click using AI-generated replacements.

**Duplicate Content Detection**
If two of your pages have the same title or description, Google
gets confused about which one to show. We find these issues
and flag them before they cost you rankings.

**Thin Content Finder**
Pages with very little content rarely rank well — but it's worse than that.
Google pre-classifies your entire site based on its weakest pages.
One thin page can suppress rankings across pages that have nothing to do with it.
We identify every under-performing page and tell you whether to expand it
or remove it — before it drags down the rest of your site.

**Broken Link Scanner**
Broken links frustrate visitors and hurt your SEO. We find every
broken link on your site automatically so you can fix them fast.

**Redirect Problem Finder**
When pages redirect through too many steps (A→B→C instead of A→C)
it slows down your site and confuses Google. We find and flag these.

**Canonical Tag Audit**
Technical checks that ensure Google knows which version of each
page to show in search results. We flag any issues automatically.

**Structured Data Checker**
Validates the behind-the-scenes code that helps Google understand
what your pages are about. Flags errors before they cause problems.

**Weekly Site Health Monitoring**
We don't just audit your site once — we check it every week.
If a new technical problem appears, we tell you right away.

**H2 Passage Quality Checker** _(new)_
Google independently scores every section of your page — not just the page as a whole.
Sections under clear, specific headings (like "How long does drain cleaning take?")
score higher than sections under vague labels (like "Our Methods").
We scan every page and flag the headings that are costing you rankings,
with one-click AI rewrites.

**Information Gain Checker** _(new)_
Google's Helpful Content system scores whether your page adds something
the user hasn't already seen. Content that just rehashes what competitors say
can get quietly demoted — even if it's well-written.
We check every page for "original elements" and warn you when a page has zero —
before Google penalises it.

**Brand Search Signal Tracker** _(new)_
When people search for your business by name, Google counts it as a trust signal
that multiplies the authority of every page on your site.
We track your branded search volume from Google Search Console and show you
whether it's growing alongside your backlinks. Growing brand searches = every
page on your site gets a bigger authority boost. We show you what's working
and what to do to grow it.
