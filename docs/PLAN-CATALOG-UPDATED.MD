### ✍️ Content Creation

**AI Focus Page Writer** 🟢
Your most important page — the one designed to bring in customers.
We analyze the top-ranking competitors and write you a fully optimized
version built to rank and convert.

**AI Article Writer** 🟢
Generate complete, ready-to-publish blog articles based on what's
already ranking on Google. Each article is written around a real
keyword your customers are searching for.

**SEO Content Briefs** 🟢
Not sure what to write about or how to structure it? Get a detailed
brief telling you exactly what headings to use, what topics to cover,
and how long the article should be — based on what Google rewards.

**Expert Content Signals** 🟢
Your articles are written to sound like they come from a real expert in your field —
not a generic AI. The more specific you are about your credentials,
the more authoritative your content sounds.
_(Set up in Project Settings → "Who is the expert behind this content?")_

**Built-In Content Editor** 🟢
Write, edit, and refine your content inside Sharkly. No copy-pasting
between tools. Everything in one place.

**Real-Time SEO Score** 🟢
As you write, you'll see a live score showing how well-optimized your content is —
scored across 5 dimensions including whether your page adds something original
that Google can't find anywhere else. A "⚠ Skyscraper Warning" tells you when
your content needs an original element to avoid the Helpful Content penalty.

**Conversion Checklist** 🟢
Every page you create is automatically checked against 8 conversion factors —
does it have the right headline, a clear call-to-action, trust signals in the
right place? The checklist adjusts based on what type of page it is.
An informational article and a service page have completely different
conversion requirements — we know the difference.

If a page is trying to sell too hard to a reader who isn't ready yet —
or not selling hard enough to someone who is — we flag it.
Most businesses lose customers because their pages don't match where
the reader is in their decision. This catches that problem automatically.

### 🔍 Content Intelligence

**Cluster Architecture Warnings** 🟢
Your content strategy is a system — and systems have failure points.
We automatically detect the 7 most common structural problems that silently
suppress rankings: articles that aren't linking where they should be, funnel
gaps that lose readers before they convert, focus pages targeting the wrong
type of keyword. You'll see them flagged before they cost you traffic.

**Keyword Cannibalization Detection** 🟢
If two of your pages are competing for the same keyword, Google splits its
ranking signals between them — and neither one wins. We catch this the moment
it happens and tell you exactly which pages are fighting each other.

**Topical Dilution Warning** 🟢
Publishing content that's completely outside your niche confuses Google
about what your site is actually about. We flag when a topic you're adding
doesn't fit your established subject area.

### 🔧 Technical SEO Health

**Full Site Audit** 🟢
We scan your entire website looking for technical problems that
are silently hurting your rankings.

**Missing Titles & Descriptions Finder** 🟢
Find every page missing a meta title or description.
Fix them all with AI-generated replacements.

**Duplicate Content Detection** 🟢
Flag pages sharing identical titles or descriptions — Google can't tell them apart.

**Thin Content Finder** 🟢
Pages with very little content rarely rank well — but it's worse than that.
Google pre-classifies your entire site based on its weakest pages.
One thin page can suppress rankings across pages that have nothing to do with it.

**Broken Link Scanner** 🟢
Find every broken link on your site automatically.

**Redirect Problem Finder** 🟢
Catch redirect chains that slow your site and lose ranking power at every hop.

**Canonical Tag Audit** 🟢
Ensures Google knows which version of each page to show in search results.

**Structured Data Checker** 🟢
Validates the behind-the-scenes code that helps Google understand your pages.

**H2 Passage Quality Checker** 🟢
Google independently scores every section of your page.
Sections under clear, specific headings score higher than sections under vague labels.
We scan every page and flag the headings costing you rankings — with one-click AI rewrites.

**Information Gain Checker** 🟢
Google's Helpful Content system scores whether your page adds something the
user hasn't already seen. We check every page for original elements and warn
you when a page has zero — before Google quietly demotes it.

**Weekly Site Health Monitoring** 🟢
We re-check your site every week. If a new problem appears, you hear about it immediately.

**Mobile-First Indexing Check** 🟢
Google ranks based on how your site looks on mobile — not desktop.
We check that your mobile version is fully functional and flag any issues that
could be causing you to rank lower than the desktop version of your own site.

**Duplicate Titles & Descriptions — Sitewide** 🟢
Scans your entire site for pages sharing the same title or description.
When pages look identical to Google, neither one ranks well.

**Pagination Canonical Check** 🟢
For stores with large product collections: ensures your paginated category
pages are correctly set up so Google doesn't treat page 2 and page 3
as separate competing pages.

**Image Optimization Audit** 🟢
Checks every image for format, file size, load order, and above-the-fold
lazy-loading issues. Slow or incorrectly loading images directly affect
your page speed score — which affects rankings.

**Intrusive Popup Detection** 🟢
Google penalizes popups that cover most of the screen on mobile.
We detect these automatically.

**Redirect Chain Depth Detection** 🟢
Every redirect hop loses ranking power. We flag chains longer than 2 hops
with specific instructions to collapse them.

**Crawl Budget Waste Detection** 🟢
Low-value pages like tag archives, author pages, and duplicate URL parameters
waste the crawl budget Google allocates to your site — meaning your real
content gets re-evaluated less frequently. We find and flag these.

**Link Velocity Monitoring** 🟢
Gaining backlinks too fast is a spam signal in Google's algorithm —
even if those links are legitimate. We track your link acquisition rate
and warn you when the pattern looks suspicious.

### 🏛️ Trust & Authority

**EEAT Scored Checklist** 🟢
Google evaluates whether your site demonstrates Expertise, Experience,
Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness — and ranks accordingly.
We give you a scored checklist across all four dimensions and tell you
exactly what to add or fix. For medical, legal, or financial businesses,
the requirements are stricter — we detect your niche and adjust automatically.

**Author Schema** 🟢
Adds structured data to your articles linking them to a specific named author
with credentials. Tells Google's entity system exactly who wrote this content
and why they're qualified.

**AggregateRating Schema** 🟢
Adds your Google review count and rating to your site's structured data —
those gold stars that appear in search results. Connects your reviews to
your business entity directly in Google's system.

**Business Entity Markup** 🟢
Links your business to all its profiles across the web — Google Business Profile,
Facebook, LinkedIn, Yelp. Tells Google's Knowledge Graph that all these
profiles belong to the same real business. Strengthens your overall authority.

**Brand Search Signal Tracker** 🟢
When people search for your business by name, Google counts it as a trust
signal that multiplies the authority of every page on your site.
We track your branded search volume and show whether it's growing alongside
your backlinks — because growing brand searches without growing links is great,
but links growing much faster than brand searches can look like artificial activity.

**IGS Domain-Level Health** 🟢
We track the ratio of your published pages that have original elements vs
those that don't. When too many pages lack originality, Google can quietly
suppress your entire site's ranking power — not just those individual pages.
We show you the site-wide picture before it becomes a problem.

## CRO ADD-ON — +$29/month

### "Turn your SEO traffic into paying customers."

### Available on any plan · +50 credits/month included · Cancel independently

---

The SEO tool gets people to your site. The CRO add-on makes sure they don't leave
without becoming customers.

There are two completely different jobs in your content system:

**SEO Anchor Pages** rank on Google. Their job is to earn the audience.
Sharkly's Workspace handles these — the UPSA score, the briefs, the entities, the linking.

**Destination Pages** convert visitors into customers. Their job is to close the sale.
CRO Studio handles these — completely separate, with completely different rules.
No word count targets. No entity lists. No SEO score. Just: does this page convert?

---

**CRO Studio — Separate Workspace** 🔜
A completely separate section of Sharkly dedicated entirely to your conversion pages —
product pages, service pages, signup pages, booking pages.
Different tool. Different score. Different rules. The absence of SEO pressure IS the point.

**Full Conversion Audit — 5 Dimensions** 🔜
We analyze your destination page across five areas:
page structure (is it in the right order?), intent match (does the CTA match where
the visitor is in their decision?), funnel coherence (are trust signals in the right place?),
psychological triggers (are the right buying instincts being activated?), and
objection removal (are the reasons not to buy being addressed before the CTA?).

**Exact Copy Fixes** 🔜
Not "add a testimonial." We write the exact sentence.
"Add this line above your CTA: 'Over 200 London homeowners trust us — see their stories below.'"
You implement it in your own CMS. We give you the words.

**Placement Instructions** 🔜
For every fix: one plain-English instruction telling you exactly where on the
page to put it. Not just what to add — where it goes and why position matters.

**Psychological Triggers Audit** 🔜
15 cognitive signals that influence purchase decisions — scarcity, social proof,
authority, reciprocity, and more. We check which ones your page is activating
and which ones it's missing, with specific copy to add each missing one.

**Objection Removal Audit** 🔜
Every potential customer has a reason not to buy. We identify the specific
objections a visitor at this funnel stage is likely to have — and check
whether your page addresses them before asking for the sale.

**Funnel Visualizer — Destination Card Unlocked** 🔜
The customer journey map shows you exactly how many people are arriving at
your destination page from your SEO content. With the CRO add-on, that card
unlocks to show your conversion score and connect directly to CRO Studio.
